Bast :: Relish Joy

Bast is an Egyptian goddess of the sun but was later adopted by the Greeks as a goddess of the moon. She was depicted with the head of a cat.

Modern Energy

As the ancestral mother of all cats, Bast is the patron of play. Bast-inspired women are energised with dancing and music, following a recipe for wholeness of body, mind and spirit. Quit subscribing to the patriarchal ‘no pain, no gain’ formula. Go with Bast on this – ‘no pain, no pain’. You can have your cake and eat it too (what is the point of having cake otherwise?) Go on, indulge yourself. The simplest things can bring you the greatest pleasure.

Do This

Do something simply wonderful today. It can be childish or adult fun, but it shouldn’t be difficult to organise. Do it again and again and make it a habit!
