Cerridwen :: Manifest Magic

The magical Welsh crone goddess Cerridwen was a shape-shifting goddess of dark prophetic powers, enchantment and divination.

Modern Energy

If you have invested energy in starting a project, you have every right to see it to fruition. Shape-shift into a magical being in order to achieve your ambition. Choose a power animal to manifest their raw energies and bring their inspiration into your efforts.
