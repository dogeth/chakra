Tara :: Practise Compassion

The goddess Tara has many different traditions. She is a Hindu symbol of eternal light and love, a Buddhist goddess of compassion teaching the wisdom of non-attachment, and a Tibetan goddess of love.

Modern Energy

Born from her mother's single tear-drop of compassion for humanity's suffering, Tara's energy is that of answering pleas for assistance. With her third eye, and eyes in each hand and foot, Tara sees beyond the mortal veil into eternity, gently reminding you that all things pass. “Each moment is perfect,” she says. Be at peace.

Do This

Give an all-encompassing Tara hug to everyone you love today. Be open to receiving them in return. You never know… maybe you’ll find it was you all along who needs the biggest dose of Tara love out of anyone!
